About

Join the journey with us

PlanetCal is changing the way people find the events and things we do in our daily life. We are re-imagining experience as an interactive social communications tool between organizations and the users. We are building platform to understand calendar events data and serve our users. Our Artificial Intelligence (AI) bots are working behind the scenes and publish recommendations based on users and interests. We want to be become planets events platform through tools for authoring events to publish to service and client experience through web and mobile applications. We aim to reduce the stress in user’s life by providing the events in their day to day life at their fingertips on any device of their choice.

We have big ambitions of becoming the planets events platform by joining the hands with all of our user’s interests. Whether you work for a company, attend a school or interested in events of your choice like sports, music etc... we want you be able to communicate with that community. You will have the opportunity to contribute the events of your interests to the group community.

At PlanetCal, we believe that time is a precious resource. We understand that every day you make a choice about how to spend your time, and who you want to spend it with. These choices are important, and sometimes it feels like we are catching up instead of actively participating. This is why we have come together to create a beautiful, easy to use tool for you to keep track of how you are managing your precious resource. We are making calendar manageable and putting you back in the driver’s seat of your busy life, so that you will never miss the events you like.

Who are we

We are bunch of individuals on a mission to provide planets best platform for events through a good user experience. We will work hard to earn your trust and make the PlanetCal a home page of choice.
